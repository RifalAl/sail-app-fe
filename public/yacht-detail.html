About Center

Ocean Pure is a stunning luxury yacht completely refitted from an authentic Phinisi ship and launched in 2017. The owner is a renowned designer and hotelier who has spared no effort ensuring that every inch of this 40-metre beauty exudes class, comfort, and cutting edge technology. From her polished teak decking to Indonesian antiques, five spacious ensuite cabins replete with mod-cons, and alluring indoor and outdoor living areas, Ocean Pure offers a lavish home away from home while you explore the magic of the Indonesian archipelago.

Onboard

Ocean Pure offers the best of both worlds with spacious living and dining areas both indoors and out. The main deck boasts a beautiful air-conditioned saloon with a bar, dining table, library, wine cellar, TV and cigar room. This space is decked out in soft sofas covered with indigo Shibori fabrics and pillows, plus striking Indonesian antiques that include tribal masks, wooden carvings and spears. Panoramic windows allow for views of the passing seascapes, and sliding bay doors open to the foredeck. The foredeck offers outdoor living at its finest with plush sunbeds, coffee tables, cushioned benches, and a long al fresco dining table for gourmet meals against a backdrop of sweeping views on nearly every side. This spectacular outdoor living space can be enjoyed on its own or connected to the indoor living and dining areas by opening the sliding doors and creating a vast 180 square metres of space.
