About Nataraja

The name, Nataraja, the Lord of Dance, comes from the Hindu connection between the spirit and the arts, offering a perfect namesake for such an inspiring and graceful Phinisi boat.

Onboard

Our 32m vessel is elegant and adventurous at heart, designed for those who pursue the spirit of freedom, curiosity and passion. It boasts a comfortable sundeck with fabulous 360° sea views to relax on during the day, share friendly moments with friends and co-travellers and from which to enjoy gazing at the star- filled tropical skies at night.

Built in 2015, the boat offers exotic and luxurious living conditions, with exquisite attention to the design detail on board, perfectly embodying the spirit of relaxed tropical life at sea. Designed for families and friends, the Nataraja’s open-living concept brings you as close to the iconic sea and landscapes of Indonesia as you can hope to get.

Cabin

All cabins on Nataraja feature air conditioning, an en- suite bathroom with hot water and basic toiletries and 220V power plugs to charge your electronics and cameras.

Our 25 m2 master cabin has two large French windows opening onto a private terrace, with direct access to the sea at the stern. Extremely spacious, it has a double bed, a single bed and a child’s bed. Our 3 standard cabins located on the lower deck have a double bed and a child’s bed.

Nataraja is the absolutely perfect place from where to enjoy a cruise with family and friends. So that you can experience the best of the Indonesian seas, the boat is equipped with well-maintained equipment for scuba- diving, snorkelling, kayaking and more.

Facility & Service

The boat is fully equipped with high-end equipment, engines and compressors, sound systems, air-conditioning in all areas and cabins and much more. Chill out in the top-deck lounge, catch some rays on the sundeck or enjoy a post-dive beer whilst watching the sunset. Two large tenders ensure ease and safety during diving.

Bespoke Cruises

Nataraja offers incredible adventures to some of Indonesia’s most fascinating landscapes both above and below the water. The Komodo Expedition takes you to the rugged islands of West Nusa Tenggara where the famous Komodo dragons reside and colourful coral reefs thrive just off the coasts, while the Raja Ampat expedition explores one of the most biologically diverse areas on Earth with thousands of untouched islands, crystal clear waters and underwater caves, cliffs and coral reefs.

No matter which expedition you choose, your cruise director will guide you on an unforgettable journey full of surprises and exclusive experiences like trips to some of the world’s most renowned snorkelling and diving sites, picnics on deserted beaches, and visits to traditional Indonesian villages where time seems to have stopped centuries ago.

Komodo Adventure
3 Days 2 Nights

A three-day sailing adventure to Komodo Archipelago, one of the most scenic parts of Indonesia’s Sunda Lesser Island will truly be an exceptional journey. The Komodo Islands are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Indonesia's most incredible destinations. The famous Komodo dragons prowl through the parched landscape while the turquoise waters that surround the islands teem with marine life. Experience all of this on a privately chartered cruise that lets you visit hidden coves and pristine beaches on a luxury vessel that you'll have all to yourself.

Trip Highlight:

  • Liveaboard experience with the luxury phinisi boat through the majestic Komodo National Park.
  • Encounter the giant lizards – komodo dragons, spot the manta rays, and relish picture perfect sunsets.
  • Snorkel or dive in one of the best dive spots and the coral reef sights in Komodo Island.
  • Snorkel over pristine coral reefs, floating in a turquoise sea and fringed by pink sand in Pink beach.

Note: Itineraries are samples only. Cruise itineraries are subject to the season, wind, current and weather around Komodo National Park.
